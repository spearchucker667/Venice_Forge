What is Venice?#

In a sentence

Venice is a privacy-first AI platform providing unrestricted access to the world's leading AI models across text, image, video, code, and audio generation.

In a paragraph

Venice is a privacy-first AI platform providing unrestricted access to the world's frontier and open-source models across text, image, video, code, and audio. Trusted by over 3 million users, Venice brings you the full power of AI without the surveillance, providing anonymized access to models from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google, alongside fully-private access to the Grok suite by xAI and leading open-source models. Venice is accessible through its app, API, and permissionlessly via staking. Venice was founded by Erik Voorhees in 2024. Learn more at venice.ai.

Use And Misuse#

Use the Venice wordmark, keys, and lockup from the approved files. Keep spacing generous, preserve the artwork, and avoid improvised marks.

Wordmark

Download wordmark
PNGSVG

The Venice wordmark should be used from approved files only. Do not redraw it, stretch it, recolor it into low-contrast combinations, or alter kerning.

Keys

Download keys
PNGSVG

The Venice keys are the compact symbol for the brand. Use them cleanly and never decorate, outline, fade, or redraw the vector.

Lockup

Download logo
PNGSVG

Keep the primary lockup intact and give it a clear exclusion zone. Do not alter proportions, opacity, curves, or supporting elements.

Colors#

Use the palette with enough contrast for the environment. Venetian Blue should be used as an accent, not a dominant background.

Secondary Accents

Correct color use

Use Deep Blue on light backgrounds and Off White on dark backgrounds. Always keep enough contrast for the logo to stay legible.

Incorrect color use

The logo only appears in Deep Blue, Off White, white, or black. Venetian Blue and the secondary palette colors are never approved logo colors.

Partnerships#

Co-branding pairs the Venice wordmark with a partner logo.
  • Place the Venice wordmark and the partner logo side by side, separated by a thin vertical divider.
  • Give both marks equal visual weight, scaling the partner logo so it reads the same size as the wordmark.
  • Leave even space on each side of the divider, and keep clear space around the whole lockup.
  • Use the wordmark color that holds contrast against the background: Off White on dark, Deep Blue on light.

Executive Bio#

Erik Voorhees#

Founder & CEO

Erik Voorhees is a technology entrepreneur and a longtime advocate for privacy and free speech. One of the earliest entrepreneurs in Bitcoin, he spent more than a decade building companies that let people move and manage money freely and privately, including ShapeShift, a pioneering digital asset exchange. He became a leading voice for the idea that individuals deserve tools they can use freely and without asking permission. Now he brings that same belief to artificial intelligence as the founder and CEO of Venice, the privacy-first, uncensored alternative to ChatGPT and Claude.

Co-Founder, President & CTO

Jesse Proudman is an engineer and entrepreneur with nearly three decades building large-scale infrastructure.
