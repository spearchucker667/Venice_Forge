# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Creative professionals, character authors, developers, and AI enthusiasts who want a local-first desktop workspace for the Venice API. They need streaming chat, image generation, research synthesis, SillyTavern-compatible character-card authoring, roleplay scripting, and creative asset management without relying on web-based interfaces. Users work on creative projects, research, content creation, interoperable character libraries, and AI experimentation in a privacy-focused environment.

## Product Purpose

Venice Forge is an unofficial local-first desktop workspace for the Venice API that provides an advanced frontend client for streaming chat, image studio pipelines, research synthesis, roleplay scripting, and local creative asset management. ST Card Studio adds preview-gated Tavern V1 and Character Card V2 JSON/PNG interoperability, lossless editing, encrypted drafts, version history, embedded/linked lorebooks, explicit AI-assisted proposals, and verified export. It offers a privacy-focused alternative to web-based AI interfaces with offline authoring and local storage of sensitive data.

## Brand Personality

Professional, innovative, privacy-focused

## Anti-references

This should NOT look like generic SaaS dashboard templates with identical card grids and standard layouts. It should avoid the "AI workflow tool" cliché of cream-colored backgrounds with gradient accents and hero-metric templates. It should not resemble generic admin panels or boilerplate Electron app designs that lack character and personality.

## Design Principles

1. **Privacy First** - Design with privacy and security as fundamental principles, ensuring sensitive data remains local and users have clear control over their information.

2. **Professional Craft** - Maintain a high standard of UI/UX design that reflects the professional nature of the tool while being approachable for creative work.

3. **Functional Focus** - Prioritize workflow efficiency and user productivity over decorative elements, ensuring the interface supports deep creative work without getting in the way.

4. **Technical Excellence** - Embrace modern development practices and tools while maintaining compatibility and performance across platforms.

5. **Consistent Experience** - Provide a unified experience across all features and functions, from chat to image generation to research tools.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance is required. Special consideration for color contrast, keyboard navigation, screen reader compatibility, and reduced motion preferences. Support for various display sizes and user customization options for text scaling and color themes.
